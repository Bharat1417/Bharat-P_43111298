Our most selling breeds



Husky


The Siberian Husky is a medium-sized working dog breed.

They are known for their thick fur coat, striking blue or multi-colored eyes, and friendly nature.

Huskies are intelligent and highly energetic, making them great companions for active individuals.

German Shepherd


The German Shepherd is a highly intelligent and versatile breed.

They are often used as police and military dogs due to their loyalty, courage, and trainability.

German Shepherds make excellent family pets and are very protective of their owners.

Labrador Retriever


The Labrador Retriever is one of the most popular dog breeds.

Labs are known for their friendly and outgoing personality.

They are great with families and excel in various activities, including service work, hunting, and companionship.

Rottweiler


Rottweilers are strong, confident dogs known for their guarding abilities.

They are highly loyal and protective of their families.

With proper training and socialization, Rottweilers can be gentle, loving, and excellent companions.
